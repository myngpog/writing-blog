This breakfast took about 30 emails sent back and forth between three differnt people to arrange; it took place in a 50's diner called the Grill that Al and Mavis loved--she even came to the diner with a thrifted wool coat from the 1950's! It was lovely, it was fun, I would do it again in a heartbeat. I *will* do it again before I graduate in May. 
<br />
It's already February 19th. February felt like it started just yesterday. 
<br />
But one small thing that lingers is the fact that I left too abruptly. When I left, it was in a half-rushed half polite way because I had to drive back to school for a meeting that took place in four minutes. I wish I didn't though--I wish, at that moment, I took my time more, even if it was at the expense of showing up just a little later to the meeting.
<br />
I wish I hugged them. But I know I'll see them again soon.
